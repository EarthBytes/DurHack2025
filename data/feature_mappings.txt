Dynamic Feature Modifiers Mapping
==================================================

WEATHER MODIFIERS:
  Sun: Fire Attack ×1.5, Water Attack ×0.5
  Rain: Water Attack ×1.5, Fire Attack ×0.5
  Sandstorm: No effect (simplified)
  Hail: No effect (simplified)
  Clear: No modifiers

STATUS MODIFIERS:
  Burn: Attack ×0.5
  Paralysis: Speed ×0.5
  None: No modifiers

TERRAIN MODIFIERS:
  Electric Terrain: Electric moves ×1.3
  Grassy Terrain: Grass moves ×1.3
  Psychic Terrain: Psychic moves ×1.3
  Misty Terrain: No effect (simplified)
  None: No modifiers

HAZARD DAMAGE:
  Stealth Rock: 12.5% of max HP
  Spikes: 12.5% of max HP
  Toxic Spikes: 6.25% of max HP + poison (simplified)
  None: No damage

TYPE ADVANTAGE MODIFIER:
  +1 advantage: +50 points
  0 neutral: 0 points
  -1 resisted: -50 points

SCORE CALCULATION:
  score = (weight_hp * effective_hp +
          weight_attack * adjusted_attack +
          weight_sp_attack * adjusted_sp_attack +
          weight_defense * defense +
          weight_sp_defense * sp_defense +
          weight_speed * adjusted_speed +
          type_advantage_modifier)

  Weights: HP=0.1, Attack=0.2, Sp.Attack=0.2, Defense=0.15, Sp.Defense=0.15, Speed=0.2
